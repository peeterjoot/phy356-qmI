Some but not all of the typos found in the second third of the book have been confirmed by Prof.\ John Sipe, who teaches the PHY456 course, also at UofT.

Any of the typos found in the last third of the text (Dirac content) and some other parts of the book were observed in private study and have not been confirmed by others.

\begin{equation}\label{eqn:desaiTyposInnards:20}
\begin{aligned}
\bra{x} e^{\frac{i}{\Hbar}(p_0 X - x_0 P)} \ket{0}
&=
\bra{x} e^{\frac{i}{\Hbar}p_0 X }
e^{-\frac{i}{\Hbar}x_0 P}

\ket{0} \\
&=
\bra{x} e^{\frac{i}{\Hbar}p_0 X }
e^{-\frac{i}{\Hbar}x_0 P}
e^{\frac{x_0 p_0}{2} }
\ket{0} \\
&=
e^{\frac{x_0 p_0}{2} }
\bra{x} e^{\frac{i}{\Hbar}p_0 X}
e^{-\frac{i}{\Hbar}x_0 P}
\ket{0} \\
&=
e^{\frac{x_0 p_0}{2} }
\left(
\bra{0}
e^{\frac{i}{\Hbar}x_0 P}
e^{-\frac{i}{\Hbar}p_0 X}
\ket{x}\right)^\conj \\
&=
e^{\frac{x_0 p_0}{2} }
\left(
\bra{0}
e^{\frac{i}{\Hbar}x_0 P}
\ket{x}
e^{-\frac{i}{\Hbar}p_0 x}
\right)^\conj \\
&=
e^{\frac{x_0 p_0}{2} } e^{\frac{i}{\Hbar}p_0 x}
\left(
\bra{0}
e^{\frac{i}{\Hbar}x_0 P}
\ket{x}
\right)^\conj \\
&=
e^{\frac{x_0 p_0}{2} } e^{\frac{i}{\Hbar}p_0 x}
\left(
\braket{0}{x - x_0}
\right)^\conj \\
&=
e^{\frac{x_0 p_0}{2} } e^{\frac{i}{\Hbar}p_0 x}
\braket{x - x_0}{0} \\
&=
e^{\frac{x_0 p_0}{2} } e^{\frac{i}{\Hbar}p_0 x}
